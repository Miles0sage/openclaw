The Art of Coffee, Since 2015

At Ember & Brew, we believe coffee is more than just a drink—it's a ritual. Our journey began in a small garage where we roasted beans over weekend fires, seeking the perfect balance of flavor and aroma.

Today, we source our beans directly from sustainable farms across the equator, ensuring fair trade practices and exceptional quality. Every cup we serve is a testament to our commitment to the craft.

We don't just serve coffee; we serve community. Whether you're here to work, relax, or catch up with friends, our doors are open wide.
